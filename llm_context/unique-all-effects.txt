## Special Abilities
- **Blood Magic** (mod): Drain lifeforce, absorb traits, cure illness, channel lifeforce
- **Combat Abilities**: Cross rivers without penalties, special battle tactics
- **Scheme Abilities**: Special scheme types, immunity to certain schemes
- **Casus Belli Access**: Special wars, invasions, and conquest options
- **Special Decisions**: Unique realm, personal, and religious decisions
- **Unique Building Access**: Special buildings and holdings
- **Title/Inheritance Effects**: Unique succession rules, title creation abilities
- **Death Prevention**: Murder attempt immunity, natural death warning
- **Faction Resistance**: Prevent vassals joining factions, reduce faction power

## Unique Limitations
- **Title Restrictions**: Cannot inherit, cannot marry, cannot purchase
- **Behavioral Restrictions**: Cannot use specific interactions or schemes
- **Disease Susceptibility**: Increased chance to spread/contract disease
- **Scheme Vulnerability**: Easier to seduce/murder/abduct

This list consolidates all significant character effects in CK3, including those from the Blood Mage mod, presenting the full range of possible values across different traits, lifestyles, and game mechanics.
